Addons in Tally

Add-ons in Tally are essentially customized programs or extensions. These Add-ons, also known as Tally Plugins, enhance Tally in various ways, boosting productivity, reliability, and overall user experience. Below is a list of popular Tally Customizations

Auto backup in Tally
The Automated Backup, also known as Auto Backup, is a feature designed to protect company data effectively. It operates seamlessly in the background, automatically initiating data backups without interrupting ongoing tasks or workflows. This ensures that data is consistently safeguarded without impeding productivity or workflow efficiency.
Alert System
The "Auto Mail" feature automates email notifications during voucher saving and for outstanding dues. This functionality streamlines communication by sending emails automatically when vouchers are saved, and it also facilitates reminders for outstanding payments, enhancing efficiency in managing financial transactions and communications.
Address Book
With this Tally add-on, you can easily disable inactive Ledgers and Item masters that you no longer wish to display in new voucher entries. However, these inactive entities will still remain visible in reports and old vouchers, ensuring historical data integrity while streamlining the data entry process for current transactions.
Ledger & Item Deactive
With this Tally add-on, you can easily disable inactive Ledgers and Item masters that you no longer wish to display in new voucher entries. However, these inactive entities will still remain visible in reports and old vouchers, ensuring historical data integrity while streamlining the data entry process for current transactions.
Voucher Approval
This add-on enables an administrator to designate a specific user as an authorizer for selected voucher types' entries made by other users. Entries made by these users will remain pending until authorized by the designated authorizer. This ensures that entries made by users are not reflected in the accounts until they are reviewed and authorized by the designated authorizer, enhancing control and accuracy in data management.
Invoice Management
This add-on feature enables users to attach relevant documents to any voucher, ledger, or stock item. Users can conveniently assign documents directly within Tally Software, and these documents can be accessed and viewed from various screens, including the Ledger Report, Voucher Screen, or Master Screen. This functionality enhances document management within Tally, streamlining access to critical information and facilitating efficient record-keeping.
Document Management System
This add-on feature enables users to attach relevant documents to any voucher, ledger, or stock item. Users can conveniently assign documents directly within Tally Software, and these documents can be accessed and viewed from various screens, including the Ledger Report, Voucher Screen, or Master Screen. This functionality enhances document management within Tally, streamlining access to critical information and facilitating efficient record-keeping.
Gridlines in Tally
This convenient Tally add-on enables the printing of grid lines in Tally reports and invoices effortlessly. Compatible with both TallyPrime and Tally.ERP9, this add-on enhances the readability of reports by adding horizontal grid lines. Whether viewing reports on screen or printing them on paper, this feature ensures ease of use and improved clarity for better data interpretation and analysis.
Negative stock
Negative inventory occurs when a manufacturer's inventory count indicates that inventory levels are below zero. There can be various reasons for negative stock, ranging from data entry errors to issues with supply chain management. Surprisingly, some companies intentionally use negative inventory as a business practice, leveraging it to optimize their operations, such as by minimizing stock holding costs or streamlining production processes. However, negative inventory can also pose significant challenges, including inaccuracies in financial reporting and potential disruptions to supply chains.
